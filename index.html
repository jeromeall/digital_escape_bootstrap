Relax with a yoga master. Learn the art of mindfulness.

Yogi Charu has studied and taught relaxation and mindfulness his entire adult life. His yoga journey began at 16 in his native Belize. Leaving home shortly thereafter, he spent most of his early career in India training in traditional Himalayan ashrams. He is an inducted disciple of the Saraswati lineage of Hatha yoga, one of the ten classical monk orders in India founded in the 8th century. Today, Yogi Charu is the master teacher of Savi Yoga in New York City, teaching public classes at The Bhakti Center and at Pure Yoga, where he is the co-head of instructor training. Pure Yoga has been voted "Best Yoga" by New York Magazine in 2009 and 2010 and is one of New York City's leading studios.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a yoga background?

Digital ^escape is for everyone! We are going to disconnect from the daily tension that can snowball into chronic stress. We will be outside a lot and eating healthy. Check out the daily schedule! The yoga itself is chill, and the meditation is passive. We are going to show you awesome ways to relax!


Really, no phones or computers? What about emergencies?

Family or coworkers can use our staff number for emergencies 24/7, but otherwise we will ask you to put up an auto-reply "I am taking a Digital ^escape this weekend, so it will have to wait until Sunday evening!" A weekend off technology feels great!


When will the first retreat be held?

We are soft-launching now while we wait for warmer weather! We are scheduling our first weekend for March 2014. Like us with Facebook below and you will be sure to get our save the date!


What is Yoga Nidra?

Yoga Nidra is the yogic art of relaxation. Lying down, the teacher will guide you through a simple series of thoughts that leaves you feeling incredibly refreshed. If you want to try Yoga Nidra you can buy a digital download on our sister site, Savi Yoga. Over the course of the weekend, you will learn to do it on your own. It is a great way to relax and the perfect introduction to meditation if you have ever been curious!


6 AM on a Saturday?! Will there be coffee?

There will be coffee! Waking up early sounds terrible, for sure, but think about it for a moment. How do you feel if you wake up late? Everything we do this weekend is about showing you how beautiful you can feel!


Do you contribute profits to charity?

Yes! (This is a yoga company). We intend to donate a material percentage of profits and we expect to announce a formal charity policy following our first retreat.


How do I get involved?

We have been getting a number of requests to get involved and we could not be more excited! We will be unrolling a Digital ^escape partners program with our launch this Spring. Please contact us via our Facebook page if you have questions!


I can't wait until Spring to escape! What can I do?

Every member of our team is a Yoga Alliance certified meditation instructor from a first-class yoga school. For more background, check out Savi Yoga and Pure Yoga, among other leading yoga schools where our teachers were certified. So if you can't wait until Spring and are interested in getting started with meditation like Yoga Nidra, send us a note on our Facebook page and we may be able to match you up with a great teacher near you!
